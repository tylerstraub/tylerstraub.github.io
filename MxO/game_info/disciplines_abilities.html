In The Matrix Online, Abilities are categorized under specialty groups called “Disciplines”. There are over 70 Disciplines in the Matrix ranging from Kung Fu Master to Network Attacker. Each Discipline is comprised of six to eight individual Abilities, many trainable for maximum power and effect. One of the remarkable things about The Matrix Online is the flexability to mix and match different disciplines together at any time. This means that you can customize and change your character's abilities anytime you need to accomplish a specific task, or simply to experience a change of pace.

The Coder
The Coder branch presents in-depth knowledge about the actual code of the Matrix itself. This knowledge allows redpills the Abilities to create and modify items inside of the Matrix. If you enjoy using pets in combat or love crafting things... click here.

The Hacker
The Hacker branch grants an understanding of how to alter the code of the Matrix for one’s own purposes, with a variety of illicit programs and utilities. If you are looking for healing skills or the Abilities to downgrade or harm enemies from afar… click here.

The Operative
The Operative branch allows for the mastery of Abilities related to both ranged and melee combat as well as stealth maneuvers. If you want to learn Kung-Fu, Akido or Karate fighting, be a Sniper, or practice the secret life of the Assasin or knife thrower... click here.
