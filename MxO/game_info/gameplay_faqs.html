    Sneak Preview of The Matrix Online!

    The Matrix Online Sneak Preview is the final phase of our rigorous beta testing schedule before officially launching the game on March 22. The Sneak Preview will begin on Saturday, March 12th at 12:00PM PST. It will end at 9PM PST on Tuesday, March 15th. In this final phase of testing, players will get a chance to see the most current version of the game, including many storyline elements from the continuation of The Matrix saga.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...


    The Matrix Online Story FAQ

    Story plays a very important part of The Matrix Online. The Machines and Zion hold to the truce, and a shaky peace holds sway. However, many organizations vie for power inside of The Matrix, and as a redpill, you will shape the future for both men and machine.
    Posted by Vosx Full Story...


    Non Player Characters FAQ

    The Matrix Online is full of colorful characters who work for various organizations, control particular neighborhoods, buy and sell resources, and even in some cases offer special missions outside of the three organizations.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...


    Mission System FAQ

    The Matrix Online contains a very robust mission system designed for maximum depth and re-playability. Players can take missions from the three main organizations, the Machines, Exiles, and Zion. Performing missions is also the primary way a player can interact with the ongoing storyline of The Matrix Online.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...


    Abilities FAQ

    One of the unique aspects of The Matrix Online Discipline Tree system is that players have the opportunity to access every Ability Code available in the game. Though players are limited in how many abilities they can load into their minds at one time, various load out combinations may be utilized by visiting the Loading Area, or any Hardline.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...


    Items & Equipment FAQ

    Items and equipment are a very large part of The Matrix Online experience. From special weapons, to tens of thousands of clothing options, items, and equipment your gear in the Matrix is one of the largest factors for success and survival.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...


    General Game FAQ

    Players of The Matrix Online (MxO) will inhabit an enormous urban sprawl, which spreads for miles in all directions. There are subways, nightclubs, skyscrapers, and dark alleys that all seem normal on the surface, but beneath this pedestrian exterior, a secret war is being waged for the survival of humanity and machine alike.
    Posted by MxO Team Full Story...
